Jazz
History

PRE 1890s

Afro roots, Worksongs, Gospels

1890

Ragtime

Syncopated melodic line and regularly accented accompaniment / player piano

1900

Blues/
Early Jazz

Small bands, originated by African Americans and in the deep south of the U.S.

1930

Swing

Less strict, syncopation gone, ]"feel" and "groove", swing beat

1945

Bebop

Some improvisation, fast beats, complex chord progressions, instruments of all types, upbeat dance

1949

Cool Jazz

Tempo slowed down more relaxed, easy listening, lighter tone

1950

Latin Jazz

Latin American rhythms, rhythms direct from Africa or African influence: Ostinato, Bossa Nova, Samba

1960

Free Jazz/
Freeform

Wide variation, improv-heavy

1969

Fusion

Combined aspects of jazz harmony and improvisation with styles such as funk, rock, rhythm and blues, and Latin jazz

1980

Contemporary

Heavily influenced by other genres such as pop and funk music
